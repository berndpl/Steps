Steps

Personal step counting that also tells you where to walk to reach 10,000.

A small iOS app, widgets, and a watchOS app — all reading Apple Health to paint your month.

Each day fills by how close you got to 10,000 steps, the way a contribution graph reads a year of commits. Pick a palette to match your home screen.

Fall short? Steps points you to a real walk that closes the gap.

Features

Your widget

Twelve palettes, tunable curves, and day shapes — the grid, painted your way.

More than steps

Rides, strength, and mindful minutes each add a badge by the day's count.

Reach your goal

Short of 10,000? A real round trip closes the gap, flown in 3D.

On your wrist

Grid, count, and badges on watchOS — places a tap from Maps directions.
